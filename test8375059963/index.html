INNOVICON is here!

BVPIEEE is organising its first ever AI conference, Innovicon. The motivation behind organising InnoviCon 2019 is to bring together top researchers and industry experts in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning and make their understanding of the recent trends accessible to undergraduates and professionals who are commencing their exploration of the field.
