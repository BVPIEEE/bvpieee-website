Core Benefits

IEEE is the world’s largest technical society, bringing Members access to the industry’s most essential technical Information, networking opportunities, career development tools, and many other exclusive benefits. As a member, you’ll be presented with new resources, valuable opportunities, and many discounts that will help you advance your career in the right direction. Through IEEE, you can find colleagues who share your vision and commitment—those who are moving technology forward today.

Knowledge – Staying current with the fast-changing world of technology…

  • IEEE Spectrum Magazine – 12 monthly issues (print) and online
  • The Institute Newsletter – 12 monthly issues (4 print, 8 online)
  • IEEE Potentials Magazine – 6 issues (online)
  • Xplore – table-of-content and abstract access to 1.2-million documents
  • Whats’s New @ IEEE – produced monthly, electronic newsletters on technical topics (10 topics to choose from)
